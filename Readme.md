# Better Taskies 🎉

***Better Taskies*** is a simple user-friendly To-Do app with your personal Yrellis assistant.

Yrellis is your friendly and helpful slime girl and she also is a mascot of this app. She believes that if you break any given big task into smaller taskies, you'll see how easy it actually is to achieve.

## ✨ Key features

* **Task Management:** Add, edit, and delete taskies with ease ✏️
* **Intuitive Controls:** Swiping is all you need to mark taskies as <kbd>Done</kbd> 👈
* **Automatic Reset:** Your taskies reset at a certain time based on their category 🔄
* **Adorable Mascot:** A multitude of cute Yrellis emotes are available to customize your experience 💖
* **System-Agnostic:** Due to it's web-based nature you can run the app on desktops as well as mobile devices 🌐

---

## ⚡ Quickstart guide

1) Put in your taskies by pressing a button with a <kbd>+</kbd> icon on it <br>They could, *for example*, be ☀️**daily**, 7️⃣**weekly** or 📅**monthly**
2) Swipe a taskie left to check it <br>Once checked you can swipe left again to uncheck it
3) Taskies automatically get unchecked once **day**, **week** or **month** ends
4) There also are 🎯**general taskies** for any general purpose you might have in mind <br>Those taskies don't reset automatically
5) Press and hold or right click a taskie to open a context menu
6) Enjoy your journey!
